WHAT'S CWOJ?

CWOJ is a free, open-source online judge solution designed for OI trainings, based on Bashu OnlineJudge. Started on Nov,20,2015, we keep seeking a better OJ solution for individuals, schools and other educational organizations.

The current and only developer, @jimmy19990, is still a novice in PHP coding. CWOJ still has a long way to go to reach our original expectations. But hey, we'll keep trying.
